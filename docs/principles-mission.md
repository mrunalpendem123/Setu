# Principles & Mission

## Mission
Define and maintain an ACP‑compatible commerce protocol for India, with Hyperswitch as the default payment handler and Indus as the reference agent implementation.

## Principles
- **Merchant of Record**: merchants remain MoR at all times.
- **PSP-agnostic**: payments can be routed via Hyperswitch or other PSPs.
- **Security first**: do not store raw card data; minimize PCI scope.
- **Local readiness**: support UPI and Indian payment flows.
- **Protocol-aligned**: follow ACP-style checkout semantics and predictable status transitions.
