# Principles & Mission

## Mission
Define and maintain an ACP‑compatible commerce protocol for India, with Razorpay as the default payment handler and Indus as the reference agent implementation.

## Principles
- **Merchant of Record**: merchants remain MoR at all times.
- **PSP-agnostic**: the protocol is PSP-agnostic; the India profile uses Razorpay as the reference implementation.
- **Security first**: do not store raw card data; minimize PCI scope.
- **Local readiness**: support UPI (Collect, Intent, QR, Reserve Pay) and Indian payment flows natively.
- **Protocol-aligned**: follow ACP-style checkout semantics and predictable status transitions.
